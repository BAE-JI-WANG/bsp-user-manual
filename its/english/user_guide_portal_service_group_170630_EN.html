Service Group

Service Portal - Service Group

The Service Group feature enables you to group all resources of operating Multi-Clouds and Hybrid environment based on service.
By re-grouping resources with the Service Group feature, you can check resource usage, cost and status of operating services.

1. Adding Service Group

  1. Click the [+] button.
  2. The “Create a New Service Group” pop-up appears. In this pop-up, enter the name of a service group to be added and click the [OK] button.
  3. The Service Group list shows that a new service group has been added.
    As you select the added Service Group, the left list shows resources added to the service group and the right one shows all resources.
  4. On the right list, select resource to be added to the selected Service Group. Then click the [{<-}] button to add it.
  5. Also, you can use Search to find resource to be added to a Service Group. As you click the [Search] button, search conditions are shown and you can search resources with the selected conditions.
  6. Click the [Save Changes] button to save changes.
    As you save changes, adding a new Service Group is completed.

2. Editing Service Group

You can change the name of a Service Group or resources included in a Service Group.

1.1 Changing Names

  1. Select a Service Group you want to rename.
  2. Click the [Edit] button next to the name of a Service Group.
  3. Change the name of a Service Group and click the [OK] button to save the new name.

1.2 Editing Resources

  1. Select a Service Group you want to edit resources.
  2. Select a resource to remove from the list of resources added to a Service Group on the left, and click the [Add] button.
  3. Select a resource to be added from the list of all resources on the right, and click the [{<-}] button.
  4. Click the [Save Changes] button to save changes.
    As you save changes, editing resources of a Service Group is completed.

3. Deleting Service Group

You can delete a Service Group.

  1. Select a Service Group you want to delete.
  2. Click the [X] button next to the name of a Service Group.
  3. The “Confirm Deleting a Service Group” pop-up appears. Click the [OK] button on the pop-up.
  4. The Service Group list shows that the selected Service Group has been deleted.
